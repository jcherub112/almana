Concierge Post-Surgical Care

Recovery, Reimagined

From the moment you leave the surgical center to the day you return to your fullest self, we are with you. Discreet, attentive, and uncompromising in care.

Private Transportation

Safe, discreet transportation from the surgical center to your home or hotel — a calm, comfortable transition after your procedure.

Overnight Care

Compassionate, professional overnight nursing to monitor and assist you through the most critical first hours of recovery.

Wellness Checks

Personalized in-person visits to evaluate healing progression, manage discomfort, and adjust your care plan as you recover.

Wound Care

Professional wound care provided in-office or in the comfort of your home, following your surgeon's post-operative protocol exactly.

Lymphatic Drainage Massage

Gentle, specialized massage therapy to reduce swelling, promote circulation, and accelerate every stage of your recovery.

Drain Removal

Sterile, gentle drain removal performed precisely to your surgeon's guidelines — comfortable, safe, and reassuring.

In-Office Aesthetics

The Art of Glow

Treatments that pair clinical precision with the slow, sensory ritual of feeling truly cared for.

HydraFacial

Deep cleansing, exfoliation, and hydration that leaves the skin immediately luminous, balanced, and refreshed.

PRP Injections — Hair & Face

Naturally rejuvenate the skin and stimulate hair growth using your body's own platelet-rich plasma.

CO₂ Laser Resurfacing

Refine fine lines, sun damage, and acne scars with precision laser resurfacing that reveals smoother, fresher skin.

BBL Photofacial

Reverse signs of aging, diminish pigmentation, and refine your skin tone with BroadBand Light therapy.

Ultrasound Therapy

Targeted ultrasound treatment to soften scar tissue, reduce post-surgical swelling, and support deeper healing.

Maderotherapy

The art of wood therapy — sculpting contours and stimulating lymphatic drainage with hand-carved wooden tools.

Cavitation Therapy

Non-invasive ultrasonic cavitation that targets stubborn, localized fat deposits with no downtime.

Vaginal Rejuvenation

Discreet, non-surgical feminine wellness treatments designed to restore comfort and confidence.

Skin Tag Removal

Safe, gentle skin tag removal for a smoother, more even complexion — performed in-office in minutes.
